Orientation Accessibility Demo

Content must not restrict its view and operation to a single display orientation, such as portrait or landscape, unless a specific orientation is essential (e.g., a piano app, game, or VR experience).

For Example Some users fix their device orientation (e.g., because their wheelchair mounts their tablet in landscape only).
Hence, the website/app should: Work both in portrait and landscape modes.

This page works in both portrait and landscape orientations.

Essential orientation example: A piano keyboard app may need landscape mode only. This is an acceptable exception per WCAG 1.3.4.
