Summary

  • Criterion: 2.2.2 Pause, Stop, Hide (Level A)
  • Goal: Allow users to control motion, blinking, or auto-updating content.
  • Good Practice: Provide clear buttons or keyboard options to pause, stop, or hide motion.
  • Bad Practice: Continuous animations or carousels without user control.
  • Exceptions: Motion is essential (e.g., progress indicator, live event data).
