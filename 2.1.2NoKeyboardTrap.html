Summary

  • Criterion: 2.1.2 No Keyboard Trap (WCAG 2.2 Level A)
  • Goal: Ensure users can navigate into and out of every component using only a keyboard.
  • Good Practice: Let focus move freely; provide ESC or Close option when trapping focus intentionally.
  • Bad Practice: Preventing Tab or Shift+Tab from leaving a region.
